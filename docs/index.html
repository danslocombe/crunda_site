Crunda is a trail across the asteroid belt.


Leap, dive and slam your way across a fractured asteroid belt powered by bespoke physics. Every landing warps and wobbles the surface, turning the environment into an unstable playground.





Explore a Dark, Comedic Wasteland


Explore a microgravity backwater abandoned by humans and inherited by a motley crew of robots. Journey from a scrap-strewn junkyard and a ruined observatory to a half-converted missionary ship and an orphanage with a sinister appetite.





Race for the Medals


Crunda is built for speedrunning and rewards momentum, routing, and precision.


  • Chase the Gold: Hunt down medals across a massive variety of levels.

  • Race the Best: Spectate and race directly against the ghosts of record holders.

  • Claim the Throne: Climb the tables to prove your routing dominance against your friends or the world.
