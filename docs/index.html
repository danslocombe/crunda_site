Crunda is a trail across the Asteroid Belt.


Leap, dive and slam your way across the route in this unique physics speedrunning platformer. Explore the mysteries of Crunda's wobbly whimsical world or challenge your friends to tackle the world record table!


  • Wobbly physics platformer - Fight against gravity and launch yourself between worlds modeled with a bespoke set of physics.
  • Explore the mysteries of the whimsical world - Uncover the stories of the motley bunch of characters inhabiting this microgravity backwater.
  • Tackle the world record table - Hunt gold medals on levels or attempt world record table. Spectate the ghosts of record runs and improve on their strategies!
